FAQs. Engaging With customers

Can I ?

1. Can I call you to arrange catering?

GruHub is responsible for forwarding the orders to the restaurants and is responsible for catering services also. However we will do that for extra charges, check specific packages; we can contact the takeaway on your behalf. You can contact us at (02) 111-11111.


2.What is GrubHub all about?

foodpanda provides customers with an easy and secure way to order food from the best restaurants in their area.

WHAT IF ?

1. What kind of restaurants do you have under your services?


We have a wide variety of culinary selections and you will be spoilt for choice. We have over 60 restaurants on foodpanda's list (and growing fast!).You can choose from Filipino, Chinese, Italian, Thai, Indian, Japanese, Middle Eastern, French and many more.

2. What are your payment methods?

We accept cash on delivery and online payments for now. We are working on other payment methods to make online food ordering as convenient as possible.

HOW DO?

1. How do I make changes to my order?

Just give us a call at (02) 111-1111 and we can change your order for you. However if your order has been dispatched already, no changes can be made.

2. How do I cancel my order?

Cancelation depends on the restaurant you place the order with. Give us a call at (02) 111-1111 and we will help you to cancel your order.
